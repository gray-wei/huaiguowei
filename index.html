CV

I am a second-year MPhil student in Robotics and Autonomous Systems at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), supervised by Jie SONG. I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science from Beijing Institute of Technology, Zhuhai. My research focuses on dexterous manipulation, with a particular emphasis on in-hand manipulation, real-world robot learning, teleoperation, and tactile sensing for contact-rich robotic tasks.

I am currently interested in building robot systems that connect human demonstrations, tactile and visual sensing, and reinforcement/imitation learning for contact-rich manipulation. My recent work includes hierarchical real-world RL for functional dexterous manipulation, goal-conditioned in-hand rotation, and cross-embodiment teleoperation with Vision Pro, Manus glove, GELLO, and several dexterous hands.
