HiFun studies hierarchical real-world reinforcement learning for functional dexterous manipulation.
The system decomposes high-DoF arm-hand control into contact-aware hand skills and skill-level coordination, targeting timing-sensitive, contact-rich tool-use tasks.

**Role:** Core contributor.

**Current status:** CoRL 2026 under review; details, figures, videos, and links will be finalized later.

**Keywords:** dexterous manipulation, real-world RL, functional tool use, Franka Research 3, XHand, Sharpa Hand.
